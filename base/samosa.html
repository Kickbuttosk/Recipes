Process

Firstly boil the potatoes.Then peel them and then smash potatoes.On the mixer or grinder make a paste of garlic,onion,ginger.

Head up the iron and pour about 5mlof oil.Then fry the mash potatoees.

Prepare a momo like ball where you will stuff the fried potatoes batter.

Take them out with a tongstick and plate

Best served while hot
